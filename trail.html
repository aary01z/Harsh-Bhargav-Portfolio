About Me

I'm Harsh Bhargav — a Graphics Designer and Motion Artist based in Mumbai. I've worked on YouTube channels, social media campaigns, and film projects, bringing ideas to life through visual storytelling.

Every frame I design is intentional. I believe great visuals don't just look good — they communicate, evoke, and linger. My work sits at the crossroads of cinema and design.

Whether you're building a YouTube brand, a social media presence, or need cinematic graphics for your film — I'm here to make it look unforgettable.

How It Happens

THE PROCESS

Every frame is intentional. Every cut has a reason.

01
Discovery

Brief & Vision

We start with a conversation. Understanding your brand, your audience, the feeling you want to create. No templates — every project begins from scratch.

◈
02
Design

Concept & Style

Mood boards, color language, typography, motion direction. Building the visual world before a single frame is animated.

◉
03
Motion

Animation & Craft

This is where it comes alive. Frame by frame, layer by layer. Timing, easing, sound sync — every detail sculpted with intention.

▣
04
Refine

Feedback & Polish

Two rounds of revisions. Your feedback shapes the output. We refine until the work feels exactly right — no rushing the art.

◐
05
Delivery

Final Output

Exported in every format you need. Ready to publish, broadcast, or drop on socials. The work leaves the studio — and makes its mark.
